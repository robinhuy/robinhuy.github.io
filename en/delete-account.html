Delete Account & Data Deletion Request

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data for any app published by Silverbit Studio (for example games or mini-apps on mobile stores).

Important: Your email must clearly state which app the request applies to (exact app name). If you use several of our apps, say whether you want one app removed or all of them. Otherwise we may not be able to match your request.

EN / VI
mark_email_unread

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

Send an email to: creative.dev.vn@gmail.com

Subject: Delete Account Request

Include the following information:

  • App name (required) — Use the exact name as shown on the App Store, Google Play, or inside the app. If you want more than one app deleted, list each app clearly.
  • Registered email address so we can verify your account.
delete_forever

Data that will be deleted

For the app (or apps) you named in your request, after we process it we will delete:

  • check_circle Account information (email, profile)
  • check_circle In-app progress and data
  • check_circle Inventory / virtual items
folder_special

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • receipt_long Transaction records (if applicable)
  • shield_lock Logs for security and fraud prevention
schedule

Processing time

  • timer Requests are processed within 7 days.
  • backup Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.
